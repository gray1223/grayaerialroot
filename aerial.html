    Delivery

    All files can be delivered digitally through Google Drive. Customers can download from the google drive link and use the footage and videos as they intend. We can also send you a USB flash drive with all media for your package.

    Size

    Images and Videos are currently taken in 16:9 Widescreen aspect ratio. All images in the galleries above are 4000x2250 16:9 Widescreen aspect ratio. Upon request in advance of filming, customers can ask for 4:3 or another common aspect ratio. Check out our youtube channel to see our projects here.

    Options

    From Pricing, an average project at $149 will provide all raw photos and videos captured, as well as an edited 1 - 3 minute portfolio video in FHD of the real estate property. If the project is for another purpose, options for delivery and filming can be customized.
